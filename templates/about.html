About Robotics Club

When did it start?

Robotics club was established in 2022

Who are we?

A team of inter-disciplinary students working on design and building Battle-Bots, RC Cars, Line Followers, Manipulators, and many more interesting projects in the robotics domain.

What are we doing?

We are a group of energetic and enthusiastic individuals currently working on our missions like:

  1. Designing and building Battle-Bots to participate in electrifying Robo-Wars, RC Cars, and Line Follower competitions.
  2. Integrating cutting-edge features like path optimization and motion planning to enhance learning and growth.
  3. Creating autonomous robots for real-world applications.
