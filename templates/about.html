About Robotics Club

When did it start?

Robotics club was established in 2022

Who are we?

A Team of Inter-disciplinary students working on Design and building Battle-Bots,RC Cars,Line followers,Manipulators and many more interesting projects in robotics domain.

What are we doing?

We are a group of energetic and enthusiastic individuals who are currently working on our missions like:

  • ▪Creating Autonomous robots
