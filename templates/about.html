About Us

Welcome to IIITDM Robotics Club

Our Mission

The IIITDM Robotics Club aims to provide a platform for students to explore and excel in the field of robotics. We focus on hands-on learning, research, and innovation in robotics and automation.

Our Vision

We envision creating a community of robotics enthusiasts who can contribute to technological advancement and solve real-world problems through innovative robotic solutions.

Our Impact

Through workshops, competitions, and projects, we have helped students develop practical skills in robotics, programming, and problem-solving. Our members have successfully participated in various national and international competitions.
